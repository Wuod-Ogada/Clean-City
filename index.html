FAQs

Can the lease period be extended?

The container lease period vary depending on the model. It is difficult to guarantee a lease extension. However, it can be negotitated.

Can a Full Container be Replaced?

We facilitate container replacement by taking the full containers, and placing an empty ones.

Can the container be collected before the lease period ends?

Yes, the response we provide to our customers extends to your request to withdraw containers upon request.

What can I do if the Municipality contacts me?

We highly advise our Customers to notify us in the event the Municipality contacts you regarding withdrawal of the container. This enables both the customer and organization avoid penalties.

Can I request for multiple containers?

Yes. We can provide our customers with more than one container. Even if it is in multiple locations

Can the container be relocated to a different location after delivery?

We have a one time placement policy. However, there are circumstances we as an organization compromise and facilitate repositioning, but not relocation.

What kind of industrial waste do you dispose?

We do not facilitate the deposition of any chemical subsance, be it in powder or packaged. We only approve of wood and plastic materials.
